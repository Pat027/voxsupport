# Acme Cloud — Plans and Pricing

## Starter — €9 / month

- 1 vCPU, 2 GB RAM, 40 GB SSD
- 1 TB egress
- Community support (forum + docs)
- Shared CPU nodes

Best for: hobby projects, static sites, early-stage demos.

## Growth — €49 / month

- 4 vCPU, 16 GB RAM, 200 GB SSD
- 2 TB egress
- 1 managed Postgres instance
- 24/5 email support (response within 8 business hours)
- Dedicated CPU nodes

Best for: small SaaS products, internal tools serving up to ~1000 users.

## Scale — €129 / month

- 8 vCPU, 32 GB RAM, 500 GB SSD
- 5 TB egress
- 3 managed Postgres instances (with pgBouncer)
- 24/7 email support (response within 4 hours)
- Managed Redis
- WAF on all endpoints

Best for: production SaaS products with paying customers.

## Enterprise — €489 / month (starting)

- Custom vCPU / RAM / storage
- Unlimited managed databases
- 24/7 phone + email support (response within 1 hour)
- Dedicated account manager
- 99.99% uptime SLA with financial credits
- Custom compliance (SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA BAA available)

Best for: regulated industries, scale-ups, production-critical workloads.

## Changing plans

Upgrades take effect immediately, prorated. Downgrades apply from the next billing cycle. Change plans via `cloud.acme.com/billing/plan` or by asking voice support.

## Cancellation

Cancel anytime from the dashboard. Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, then permanently deleted.
